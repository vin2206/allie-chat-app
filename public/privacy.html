Privacy Policy

Shraddha Chat uses Google Sign-In to identify users. We collect and store your Google account email and unique ID (sub) to create your account, apply the one-time bonus, and prevent abuse.

What we collect

  • Basic Google identity info from the Google button: email, name, profile picture (if provided), and the unique ID (sub).
  • On your device (localStorage): coin balance and simple settings.
  • If you use voice features: we process audio to provide transcription and voice replies.

How we use data

  • Authentication and session management.
  • Granting a first-time coin bonus and enforcing fair-use (no temp-mail farming).
  • Operating features like speech-to-text and text-to-speech.

What we do not do

  • We do not access your Gmail, Drive, Contacts, or other Google data.
  • We do not sell personal data.

Third-party processors

We use Google Identity Services (sign-in). Voice and AI processing may use providers such as OpenAI/OpenRouter and ElevenLabs, and email delivery may use Resend.

Retention & security

We keep essential account identifiers (email and sub). Audio and logs may be retained temporarily to operate and troubleshoot the service. We apply reasonable safeguards but no method is 100% secure.

Age

Service intended for users 16+.
